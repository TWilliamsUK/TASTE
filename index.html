Welcome!

Taste restaurant is open to the public during term-time, Monday to Friday. All dishes are freshly prepared by our highly skilled hospitality and catering students, giving you the opportunity to sample food prepared by the next generation of star chefs and hospitality professionals.

Our facilities are of the highest quality, and have been tried and tested by some of the best! Top chefs and celebrities including Brian Turner, Mark Hix, Jamie Oliver, Atul Kochar and Gordon Ramsay have all used our kitchens in recent years. We recently hosted the BCA Chef of the Year Competition.

Booking

Our new restaurant seats 70 and is open 7 days a week. We fill up fast so to avoid dissapointment we recommend booking early.

Booking a table online is easy and takes just a couple of minutes. Please let us know your party size and the date and time you require by filling out the form below.

We also offer catering for private events. For larger groups we do suggest booking atleast four weeks prior to your desired date though we'll always do our best to accomodate you, even at shorter notice.
